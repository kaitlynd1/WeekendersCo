Our Story

We started Weekenders Co. because we wanted a reminder — and a product — that made it easier to choose play over polish. Dogs do this without apology: they run, fall, shake it off, and ask for more. That's the kind of weekend we design for.

What we care about

Good materials. Clean design. Things that feel reliably effortless. The beanie you wear while driving up for a sunrise ski, the cap you throw on for a foggy beach walk — small rituals that turn time off into something you actually remember.

Why dogs?

They remind us that leisure isn't a luxury — it's a practice. Dogs play with reckless sincerity. We put them in human situations because doing human things should be as honest and joyful as a game of fetch.

How we launch

We begin with one product at a time so we can keep the details right: the stitch, the fit, the feel. We test in the wild (real weekends, real people), then iterate. If you want more background on production or materials, email orders@weekendersco.com.
