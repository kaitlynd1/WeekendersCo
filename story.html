Our Story: Adventure Built From Scratch

Weekenders Co. is a family company built on an old promise: a life lived outside.

For as long as I can remember, my dad has been my adventure guide. He always made sure our dog and I were packed up and ready to go, whether it was finding fresh powder on a mountain, tackling a new bike trail, or jumping into the nearest lake. He taught me about the unconditional joys of the outdoors and the simple bliss of a weekend spent having fun.

But as I looked in my closet, I saw clothes that felt generic and did not match the memories we were making. I wanted gear that was as authentic, durable, and fun as those family adventures, perfectly expressing that feeling of freedom you get when your dog is running full tilt ahead of you, living completely in the moment.

The Launch: Family Driven Quality

So, this year, I decided to start Weekenders Co. This is more than a business idea; it is a family commitment to the lifestyle we love.

As a new company, we are starting small, but we are relentless about quality. We know that the best adventures require reliable gear, so we are focused on sourcing higher quality materials that are designed to last. Every piece we create has to be tough enough for the trail, comfortable enough for the drive home, and meaningful enough to become a part of your own adventure story.

Built for Freedom and Fur

We believe that nothing expresses love for freedom and fun better than a dog. They are our ultimate companions, always ready to go and always reminding us to keep things simple. Our clothes are designed to be lived in, enjoyed, and shared on every journey with your best friend.

Thank you for joining us on this brand new journey. We can't wait to see where you take your Weekenders gear.

— Kaitlyn Donato & Family, Weekenders Co.
