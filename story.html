Our Family Story

We started Weekenders Co. because the people we love were already teaching us how to spend our time: outside, roughing it a little, laughing a lot. Dogs do this best — they don’t wait for the perfect conditions, they make the moment. That’s the engine behind every product we design.

What matters to us

Simple things done well: durable materials, thoughtful fit, and gear that looks at home on a trailhead or a coffee shop. We make pieces you’ll actually use, not something to file away as “nice.”

Family-first design

This is a small, family-focused company. We ask real people (our siblings, parents, cousins) to test everything, wear it for a season, and tell us what to fix. The Summit Beanie started at a kitchen table and became what it is because our family refused to let it feel flimsy.
